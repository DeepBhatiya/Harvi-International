Technical Notes

Water Absorption: Classified as BIa (≤0.5%) according to EN 14411, making tiles suitable for both indoor and outdoor use, including freeze-thaw conditions.

Breaking Strength: Minimum breaking strength of 1300N ensures durability and resistance to impact during installation and use.

Chemical Resistance: UA (unaffected by acids), ULA (unaffected by alkalis), UHA (unaffected by household chemicals) rating ensures long-term performance.

Fire Rating: Class A1 fire resistance classification (non-combustible) meets highest safety standards for commercial and residential applications.
