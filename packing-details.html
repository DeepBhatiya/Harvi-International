Packaging Standards

Box Specifications

  • Corrugated cardboard boxes with high strength
  • Waterproof inner lining for protection
  • Corner protectors on each tile
  • Clear labeling with product code and specifications
  • Stacking instructions printed on box
Pallet Information

Pallet Specifications

  • Standard pallet size: 120cm x 100cm
  • Maximum pallet height: 180cm
  • Stretch wrap protection for stability
  • Corner boards for additional protection
  • Pallet label with complete product information
Important Notes

Storage: Store in a dry, well-ventilated area. Keep boxes flat and avoid stacking beyond recommended height.

Handling: Use proper lifting equipment. Do not drop boxes or handle roughly to prevent tile breakage.

Transportation: Secure pallets properly during transit. Protect from moisture and extreme temperatures.

Quality: All tiles are inspected before packing. Report any damage immediately upon receipt.
