What types of tiles do you manufacture and export?

We specialize in porcelain and ceramic tiles including polished, matt, glazed, wooden, and large-format slabs suitable for residential and commercial projects.

What tile sizes and thicknesses are available?

We offer a wide range of sizes such as 60×60, 60×120, 80×160, and large slabs with multiple thickness options to match different applications.

Do you provide customized designs and finishes?

Yes, we support custom designs, surface finishes, and packaging options based on client requirements and export market standards.

Which countries do you export tiles to?

We export our tiles to multiple international markets including the Middle East, Europe, Africa, and Asia, following global quality standards.

How do you ensure tile quality and packaging?

Each tile undergoes strict quality checks, and we use export-grade packaging to ensure safe delivery and damage-free transportation.
